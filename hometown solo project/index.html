Top Activities to Enjoy in Accra

Relax at Labadi Beach

Soak up the sun, enjoy fresh seafood, and experience vibrant nightlife at Accra's most popular beach.

Shop at Makola Market

Explore the bustling market where you can find colorful textiles, handmade crafts, and local spices.

Visit Kwame Nkrumah Mausoleum

Learn about Ghana's first president and the country's rich history at this iconic landmark.
